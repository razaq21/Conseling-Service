    Al Mashura IIUM offer a variety of services aimed at helping the client resolve personal problems and help to improve an individual's personal skills to encourage better behavior functions and features to generate superior personal character.

    Providing psychological and career management services for clients (students and staff).

    MISSION

    Striving to provide management services involving psychological counseling, consultation, test administration and career guidance to clients to encourage functional behaviors through the management of client emotional, mental and physical.
